These are series of maps about Victorian Heavy Vehicle Routes,

Strategic Local Roads of Horsham and Various assets information

The following is the Heavy Vehicle Route Map of Victoria

This map provides interactive informations that you need to know if you are trying to plan truck route trip. It details about various roads with information on

what vehicle are allowed. It also provides information about restricted bridges and capacities

This map is created using Azure PostgreSQL and CARTO. The widgets on the right side will also provide information about how many m of roads are restricted or conditionally apporved for a given truck

The following map shows the Transport assets of Horsham and Strategic Future Frieght Route

This map details all roads of Horsham local government area.There are over 3000 km of roads in Horsham; out of which there are some roads which works as strategic route

which council is working to widen or strengthen over next 5-20 years. These roads are key routes that helps to connect farm produces, dairy or meat products to nearest

intermodal hubs or silo. These maps were produced by Council in conjuction with 5 neighbouring council and local community

The widget on the right side of the map provides insight about various information about length of roads in different hierarcy and surface types

The following map shows miscellaneous assets and services at Horsham LGA

Horsham Council is looking to find road making materials closeby to enable economically management of road assets of Horsham. Some potential gravel pit sites has been

displayed. Also, all kerb are mapped this will help council to look into what area of roads doesn't have kerb at present. There are several drinking fountains around the

municipality. These drinking fountain map will help community to quickly and conveniently access these facilities

The widget on the right side of the map provides insight about numbers of services or sites

The following is the building map of Horsham

Hover over the map to get the details.This map is created using QGIS2Web and hosted via Github

There are several buildings and facilities to sever the community and winder Wimmera Malee region. However, not all buildings are council's. From the community

perspective though, all buildings belongs to council.This map is created with aim to let community know all buildings or facilities that Horsham Council has responsbility

on. This reduces many customer calls from user groups to council. It is also useful for new users to know where these facilities are located.

The following is the Capital works program of Horsham for 21-22 financial year

The aim of the following web map is to inform the community where council is spending it's capital budget. This gives visibility and tranparency about what

is going to happen on various assets and services
